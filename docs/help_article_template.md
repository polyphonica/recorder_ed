## Troubleshooting

### Payment failed or didn't go through?

- **Check your payment details** for typos
- **Ensure you have sufficient funds** or credit available
- **Try a different card** if the issue persists
- **Contact your bank** - they may have blocked the transaction
- Still having issues? [Create a support ticket](#)

### Don't see the course in "My Courses"?

- **Wait a few minutes** - processing can take 1-2 minutes
- **Check your email** for a confirmation
- **Try logging out and back in** to refresh your session
- Still not showing? [Contact support](#) with your order number

See our article: [How to Request a Course Refund](#)
