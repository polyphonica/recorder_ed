What is a Guardian Account?

As a guardian, you can:

  • Manage accounts for multiple children under 18
  • Book and pay for lessons, courses, and workshops on their behalf
  • Track their progress and learning activities
  • Receive all communications about their education
  • Transfer accounts to them when they turn 18
